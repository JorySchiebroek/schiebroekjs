Context

As finishing touch to my education, I was in an internship for the Dutch National Tax Authority (Belastingdienst) together with a fellow student. Part of this big government authority is the Dutch Customs (Douane), which takes care of border control. My graduate assignment was to come up with a solution that would aid the customs personell with scanning substances intercepted in package mail.
